Game Title: Shooter

Description:

Shooter is a fast-paced arcade shooter that puts you in control of a lone starship patrolling the outer reaches of the solar system. Your mission is simple but challenging: defend your ship from waves of incoming alien saucers.

You control a powerful spacecraft that stays fixed at the bottom of the screen. From the top and sides, hostile flying saucers descend toward you. Your goal is to aim and shoot them down before they collide with your ship.

How to Win:

Every time you destroy an alien saucer, you earn 1 point.

When you destroy 10 saucers, you win the game.

How to Lose:

Each saucer that reaches your ship and crashes into it counts as a missed enemy.

If you miss 5 saucers (meaning five of them successfully hit your ship), your defenses are overwhelmed and the game ends in defeat.

Key Features:

Simple one-button shooting mechanics — easy to learn, hard to master.

Increasing speed: the more saucers you destroy, the faster the next ones fly.

No health bars — just a clear counter: 10 kills to win, 5 misses to lose.

Classic pixel-art visuals with retro sound effects.

Example Gameplay Flow:

Saucer 1 → Shot down. Score: 1.
Saucer 2 → Shot down. Score: 2.
Saucer 3 → Missed! Misses: 1.
…
Score reaches 10 → Victory!
Or Misses reach 5 → Game Over.
